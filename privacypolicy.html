Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2025

TwoArrowsUP develops and publishes mobile games and apps, including RootFather and future titles. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our games, apps and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Advertising: Our games use third-party advertising providers (such as Google AdMob) which may collect device identifiers and usage data to show personalized or contextual ads.
  • Purchases: When you buy in-app items (e.g. “Leaf” premium currency), the purchase is processed securely by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store your credit card or billing information.
  • Gameplay Data: We may store progress, scores, achievements and items locally on your device and in secure cloud storage to allow saving and restoring progress.
  • Website: Our website (twoarrowsup.com) may collect anonymous analytics or standard server logs (IP address, browser type, etc.).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core gameplay, save progress and enable features such as achievements.
  • To show ads and reward users for viewing ads.
  • To process in-app purchases.
  • To improve our products and detect errors or abuse.

3. Children’s Privacy

Our games are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g. EU/EEA under GDPR or California under CCPA), you may have rights to access, delete or opt-out of data processing. Please contact us at info@twoarrowsup.com for requests.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Limited data may be shared with third-party providers (AdMob, Unity Services, analytics) strictly for delivering ads, purchases, or app services.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted at this page with a new “Effective date”.
